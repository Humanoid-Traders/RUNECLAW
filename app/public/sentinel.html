Systemic Risk Sentinel

A market-wide crowding radar. When the whole USDT-perp universe leans one way, funding runs hot, fresh leverage piles in and moves start correlating, a coordinated unwind — a liquidation cascade — gets more likely. The Sentinel reads those conditions across the market from public data and flags them. Heuristic reads, not a verdict and not investment advice.

Public Bitget market data (funding, open interest, ΔOI, 24h direction) aggregated across the perp universe. Open interest shown is a market figure — a public fact, never anyone's account or P&L. The gauge and flags are heuristic crowding reads, not predictions or signals; positioning can stay crowded for a long time. Build your own safety envelope in the app.
