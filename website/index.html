What is Runeclaw

AI Trading Command Core

RUNECLAW is an AI-powered trading command system that scans markets, generates trade ideas with confluence scoring, and enforces disciplined execution through a 21-check fail-closed risk engine.


Every decision is logged. Every trade requires human confirmation. Paper trading is the default. If anything is uncertain, the answer is always no.

Safety Framework

Governed by Discipline

The safest trade is no trade. Every guardrail exists to protect capital first.

🔒
Simulation First
Paper trading is the default mode. Live execution requires explicit double-unlock via environment config.
DEFAULT ON
✋
Human Confirmation
No trade executes without explicit human approval through Telegram inline keyboard.
ENFORCED
🚫
Fail-Closed Design
Any risk check failure, error, or uncertainty results in automatic trade rejection. No exceptions.
CRITICAL
⚡
Circuit Breaker
Trading automatically halts when daily loss exceeds 5% or drawdown exceeds 10%.
AUTO-HALT
📏
Position Limits
Maximum 2% of portfolio per position. Maximum 5 concurrent positions. Hard-coded limits.
ENFORCED

Full Audit Trail
Every decision, rejection, and execution is logged in structured JSON with timestamps and reasoning.
