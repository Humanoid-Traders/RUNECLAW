What is Runeclaw

Unleash the Power of
Intelligent Trading

RUNECLAW is an elite AI trading command system built to hunt precision setups and enforce disciplined execution. It merges multi-timeframe analysis, confluence scoring, regime detection, macro awareness, and risk-first logic into a battle-forged framework.


Every decision is logged. Every trade requires human confirmation. Paper trading is the default. The risk engine is fail-closed: if anything is uncertain, the answer is always no.

Every Decision Transparent

RUNECLAW doesn't just trade — it explains every decision in structured, auditable detail. Every signal source, every indicator vote, every confidence score is exposed.

Each trade idea includes a full breakdown of the decision chain: the source signals, regime context, indicator consensus, blended confidence, and precise entry/exit levels with risk-reward calculation.


This isn't a black box. The LLM thesis, technical confluence, and regime detection are all surfaced as structured data — visible in Telegram, logged in JSON, and available for audit.
